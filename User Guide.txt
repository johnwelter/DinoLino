CURVATURE MODE: 

A geometric calculation to calculate curvature by approximating a curve as the arc of a circle using three points. 

Click twice to draw a line from each end of the curve, then click a third time in the midpoint of the curve. Click a fourth time to begin another curvature calculation.

Angle: the central angle of a circular arc drawn using these three points. 
Aspect ratio: the length of the first line drawn divided by length of the second line.
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
ANGLE MODE:

Draws a triangle on the image and returns its three interior angles. 

Click three times anywhere on the image to create a triangle. Click a fourth time to begin drawing another triangle.

Angle A: The angle at vertex A.
Angle B: The angle at vertex B.
Angle C: The angle at vertex C.
